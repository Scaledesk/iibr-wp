INTERNATIONAL INTERNSHIP PROGRAMME
USA & Mexico

Industrial Internships Abroad Offer Students Global Experience:

Industrial internships in foreign countries offer our students an international perspective. This programme has been designed keeping in mind that the challenges that the current corporate generation face are global in nature.

Students Will Be Given A Course Certificate From The Foreign University/Institute As Per Their Chosen Module:

Moving away from the “study tour” concept, ASM’s internships abroad culminate in a formal certification at the end of the programme.

Opportunity To Learn In Two Different Corporate Environments, And Become An Internationally Qualified Professional:

Business practice and philosophies are generally geo-centric, and in today’s environment it becomes crucial that the student gains a diverse perspective, thereby imbibing business practices, procedures, values and ethics from a culture radically diverse from their own. If students meet the relevant criteria they will be considered for an international internship and will spend 3 months as a paid employee in the host organization.
